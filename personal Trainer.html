Nutrition Tips

Pre- Workout Nutrition tips:"Eat a balanced meal 1-2 hours before your workout. If you're short on time, a smaller snack 30-60 minutes prior can work.Choose easily digestible sources like oatmeal, whole grain bread, or fruit to provide sustained energy. Include lean proteins like yogurt, chicken, or a protein shake to support muscle function and repair. Drink water to stay hydrated and enhance performance."

Post-Workout Nutrition"Eat within 30-60 minutes after your workout to maximize recovery and muscle repair. Consume high-quality protein like eggs, fish, or a protein shake to aid muscle recovery and growth. Include carbs like brown rice, sweet potatoes, or fruit to replenish glycogen stores and support recovery. Rehydrate with water or an electrolyte-rich drink to replace fluids lost during exercise."
